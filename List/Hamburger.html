Hamburger Potato

Time: 20 mins
Total Time: 1 hr 40 mins
Servings: 6
Yield: 6 Servings

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces egg noodles
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 3 tablespoons flour
  • 3 ½ cups cold milk
  • 1 (10 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • ½ cup diced red bell pepper
  • ½ cup diced green bell pepper
  • ¼ cup chopped green onions
  • 5 ounces crushed potato chips

Directions

  1. Cook the beef and drain the grease.
  2. Mix the soup, milk, and seasonings.
  3. Layer the potatoes, soup mixture, and beef in the prepared baking dish.
  4. Bake until the potatoes are tender.
  5. Top with cheese and continue baking until the cheese is melted.
  6. Stir pepperjack cheese mixture, turkey meat, and noodles in a large bowl until evenly incorporated. Pour mixture into a large casserole dish. Sprinkle chips on top, pressing down slightly with the tines of a fork. Bake in the preheated oven until casserole top is golden brown and sauce is bubbling, about 30 minutes.
